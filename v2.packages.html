Introducing Packages

Buy a bundle of discounted rides or kilometres and save BIG.

What are Packages?

Packages are a bundle of rides or kilometres offered at a discount. When you buy a package, you pay for your discounted rides or KMs up front and save BIG in the long run! It’s the best way to save on daily transportation.

We have two types of packages to suit your specific needs:

Ride packages KM packages

Why should I use Packages?

Ride packages

Pay up front for a set number of discounted rides. For any ride that costs less than the pre-defined discount, you pay nothing. Otherwise, you’ll only be charged the difference between your ride cost and the discount amount.

Pay upfront and save BIG

Predictable prices make it easier to plan your monthly expenses.

Get a discount on the total ride cost

All your rides are automatically discounted just like a promo code.

Priority customer support

Only the best Customer Service for our VIPs!

KM packages

Pay up front for a set number of kilometres. Your kilometres will be deducted for each ride based on how far you travelled.

Pay once and pay less.

Pay one upfront payment for all your rides at a discounted rate.

No peak. No surcharges.

No price increase during peak time, and no upfront charges for airport pickups.

Priority customer support.

Only the best Customer Service for our VIPs!

How do I buy a package?

It’s easy! You can buy Packages right through the app. You can pay using a credit or debit card, or Careem credit, which you can top-up by paying your captain extra cash after a trip, by redeeming Careem Rewards points or through Fawry, Easypaisa or other payment channels*. Be sure to turn on the “auto renew” option while checking out to automatically repurchase the same Package when yours runs out!

*Only available in certain markets.
